CausaliDox

March 17, 2010

CausaliDox is Scarum

Causality extrapolated to Paradox.

Becoming your own antipod.

Drowning in neurotransmitters.

Succumb to internal stimuli.

Skewed interpretations.

Unwilling to collapse the wave function. CausaliDox is a vehicle for exploration, reaching the outer limits of noise and beyond to the vast calmness of soundscapes, voyages through forests of melody and storms of percussion, driven by a fascination for the intricate undeniable bonds between causality and paradox.
