Did You Have A Name?

November 3, 2010

Open doors
can lead you
to places
you never pictured yourself
to be

a strain on the mind
the thinking of the free

She opened her face
and swallowed the doubt

she left no room for understanding
she left no room for hope
she left no room for understanding
she left no room for choice

all she ever needed was just some new toys

Did you have a name?

She kicked open this door
I went in to see if she was o.k.
She wasn't there
But I had to stay
