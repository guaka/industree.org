Lovely brain machine
you bring about such lovely views
you entertain so well
you plague the eye with things unseen
and harrass the ear with sounds that have never been

Lovely brain machine, HAIL

of many destinations shrouded in haze

You are all in one
Lovely brain machine

You are one in me
I am everything
