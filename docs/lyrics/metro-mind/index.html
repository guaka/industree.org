Metro Mind

November 2, 2010

My eyes and the rain
fell onto the floor
I hoped for summer
but you asked for even more
So I left
you right

understand that we're all alone

the beats and her breath
the answering void
legalize confusion
kill the doubt

are we able
are we stable
tell your fable
plug in the cable

and
head on
