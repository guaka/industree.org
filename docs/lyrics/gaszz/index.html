GASZZ

November 3, 2010

It's been a long time
since I concsiously
breathed cold, autumn air
too long I have been inside my head
looking out into a hostile world
drawn back...resisting...a forcefull world
my own strength has dimished
any magic that was left
no so badly needed
vapourized into the air
